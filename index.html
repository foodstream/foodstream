foodstream

We believe edible food should be eaten.

foodstream provides a platform for individuals and institutions to offer their excess food that is edible but unmarketable so that other users may put it to use.

Have excess food that is still edible? Check out the foodstream app and join the movement to reduce food waste!

2301 tons of food waste are disposed of per day in NC alone, yet 1 in 4 children and 1 in 5 adults are at risk for hunger in our state.

foodstream has a solution.

1. Sign up


Easily sign up with an email address to offer your edible but unmarketable food or claim food from other suppliers. Once signed up, you can edit your personal information and upload a profile photo.

2. Post


Our simple interface allows you to quickly create posts for unwanted food and upload an image of the food being offered. Built-in messaging functionality allows for easy communication with individuals who claim your post.

3. Claim


Search by distance to find posts near you based on your current location. Claim any available food by clicking "Claim" on the post. Get directions to a claimed post and add the pick-up time to your calendar using built-in Google Maps and ical functionality.
